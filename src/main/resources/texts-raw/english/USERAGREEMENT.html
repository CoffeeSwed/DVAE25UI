How is my privacy protected?

To maintain your privacy, all the collected data sent from your device to us is protected by LDP (Local Differential Privacy). LDP works by inserting noise into the data before it is sent. Noise for this application is introduced by randomly modifying the original data into a state that differs to some degree from the original data.

This allows us to publish a large amount of complex statistics to our different stakeholders while making it difficult for them to be certain that you are a customer. LDP also prevents anyone with the modified data to be certain about what the original data was.

The average amount of data that is modified is balanced between privacy and the number of users. This is because adding more noise increases privacy, but also the number of user needed to obtain useful statistics.
