How is my privacy protected?

To maintain your privacy, all the collected data sent from your device to us is protected by LDP (Local Differential Privacy). LDP works by inserting noise into the data it is sent. Noise for this application is introduced by randomly modifying the original data into a state that differs to a degree from the original data.
This allows us to publish statistics to our different shareholders while making it impossible for them to determine if you are a customer whilst also preventing anyone with the modified data to be certain about what the original data was.
